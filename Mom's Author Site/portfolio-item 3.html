Book:If Our Table Could Talk

Stories from the Table

What's the difference between a dining room table and a treasured heirloom? Lots of memories!

The family history of our beloved dining room table is now completed and has been a long time coming! This book attempts to chronicle the history of a treasured family heirloom that has found its way into our homes and our family's lives over the past 60plus years. I hope you enjoy following along with us and become inspired to create your own memories with that piece of furniture, jewelry, pictures, clothes or whatever it may be that has somehow found its way into your life.

>>Buy it Now<<

Hopefully, when our kids, grandchildren and future descendants peruse these pages, they will come to understand a little more about our family than just some names and dates on a family tree!

We lived … we endured loss … we laughed … but more importantly, we loved! Thanks for sharing this piece of family history with us!
